Legal Tools

If you are dealing with harassment, abuse, or a custody dispute, California courts provide legal tools to help protect you and your family. Find and download the forms you need to get started.

Restraining Orders

A restraining order can legally require someone to stay away from you, your home, or your workplace. Use the links below to access California court forms for filing a civil harassment or domestic violence restraining order.

Protective Orders

A protective order is issued by a court to protect victims of abuse, stalking, or threats. Download the forms needed to request an emergency, temporary, or permanent protective order in California.

Custody Forms

If you need to establish, modify, or enforce a custody or visitation arrangement, California courts require specific forms depending on your situation. Access the correct forms for your case here.

Safety Planning

Having a plan in place before a crisis happens can make a critical difference in your safety and the safety of your children. This section provides a go bag checklist and a step-by-step guide to building a personal safety plan tailored to your situation.
